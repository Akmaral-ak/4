White shark

white shark facts

Facts:
1) The white shark is the largest modern predatory fish known as the great white shark and the man-eating shark.
2) The gestation period of white sharks is 11 months. Cubs' powerful jaws begin to flutter in the first month.
3) Unborn sharks cannibals: stronger cubs eat the weaker ones right in the uterus. Birth occurs in spring or summer.
4) The white shark ranks fourth among the most dangerous creatures on our planet.
5) There is a known case when an aggressive great white shark broke through the bottom of a sailing yacht. The owner of the ship and his partner were rescued in time by colleagues who came to the rescue. The yacht sank.
